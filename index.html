Who We Are

One Foundation,
Countless Lives Changed

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation operates across India from Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh — a youth-powered movement dedicated to lasting, measurable social change.

Section 8 Licensed 80G & 12A Certified ISO 9001:2015 NGO Darpan – NITI Aayog CSR-1 Registered

My vision is to unite driven individuals to create lasting, positive change. Every step I take is toward building a more inclusive and brighter future for everyone.

— Mr. Govind Shukla, Founder & CEO

Social Impact

Why This
Work Matters

Every initiative creates measurable, life-changing outcomes for the communities that need it most, across the length and breadth of India.

  • ✓Over 50,000+ beneficiaries supported directly across 28 states in India.
  • ✓20,000+ saplings planted under Project Prakriti, fighting deforestation.
  • ✓900+ women gaining financial independence through Project Udaan.
  • ✓Hundreds of children given access to quality education via BachpanSala.
  • ✓50+ stray animals rescued and fed daily through Project Jeev.
  • ✓Youth-powered model ensures authentic, on-ground, passionate action.
