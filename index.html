Who We Are

One Foundation, Countless Lives Changed

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla. Licensed by the Central Government under Section 8 of the Companies Act 2013, we operate across India from our base in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

Section 8 Licensed 80G & 12A Certified ISO 9001:2015 NGO Darpan – NITI Aayog CSR-1 Registered
My vision is to unite driven individuals to create lasting, positive change in society. Every effort I make is a step toward building a more inclusive and brighter future for everyone.
— Mr. Govind Shukla, Founder & CEO
Our Work

Six Projects. One Mission.

Each initiative addresses a critical gap in society — from hunger and illiteracy to animal suffering and environmental damage.

🍱
Project Seva

Distributing nutritious meals and essential clothing to impoverished communities. Over 50,000+ meals served, ensuring no family goes hungry.

Food & Clothing
📚
Project BachpanSala

Nurturing young minds by providing quality education, digital literacy, and school supplies to underprivileged children across India.

Education
👩
Project Udaan

Empowering 900+ women through skill development, vocational training, and financial independence via self-help groups in rural areas.

Women Empowerment
🐾
Project Jeev

Rescuing and feeding 50+ stray animals daily. Engaging youth to spread compassion and build an inclusive, empathetic society.

Animal Welfare
🌿
Project Prakriti

Planting 20,000+ trees, organising clean-up campaigns and conservation workshops. Every tree is a gift to future generations.

Environment
🔧
Project Vikas

1,000+ internship opportunities across digital marketing, content writing, HR, events and more — bridging education and employability.

Social Impact

Why This Work Matters

Every initiative creates measurable, life-changing outcomes for communities that need it most.

  • Over 50,000+ beneficiaries have received direct support through our projects across 28 states in India.
  • 20,000+ trees planted under Project Prakriti, contributing to cleaner air and climate resilience.
  • Hundreds of underprivileged children now have access to quality education through BachpanSala.
  • 900+ women gaining financial independence through Project Udaan’s skill-building programs.
  • 50+ stray animals rescued, fed, and cared for every day through Project Jeev.
  • Youth-led model ensures campaigns are driven by energy, creativity, and genuine passion.
